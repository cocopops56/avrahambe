About

Through my acrylic paintings, I engage in a dialogue with light, color, and texture. From the ancient walls of Jerusalem to the electric nights of cities, and the symbolic light of the Menorah, I seek the hidden energy within places and objects. My brushes dance across the canvas, leaving visible, thick marks, allowing the paint itself to tell a story of emotion and vibration.
